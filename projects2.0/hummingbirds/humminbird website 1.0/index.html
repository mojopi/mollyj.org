Hummingbirds
Getting to know Hummingbirds…
Attempts at portraiture in my neighborhood

I recently moved to San Diego and am fixated on hearing hummingbird calls. On my bike to work. On walks to the beach. En route to the grocery store. I hear them everywhere and I'm obsessed with these magical creatures. Their small size, boldness, speedy dives and displays, shimmering feathers, the sound of their wings — their whole gestault speaks to my soul. I want to get to know them and to see them upclose so I've started to photograph them.

Here is my journal of what I've done and learned each day.
