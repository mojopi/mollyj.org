Open Questions

Right now, it doesn't feel like my work is connected with a big idea (e.g. evolution). How do I think about this?
Will evolution start to come into my work more as my relationships with animals grow and my questions become more nuanced (e.g. snail shell project idea). Or is it just not the right frame to push right now? What do I do about this?

How does having a public forum to share documentation around animals make (or not make) me able to see them more closely? How to I protect observing over documenting or is that even a real question?

(I’ve been spending a lot of time generating stuff around the animal’s I’m observing but haven’t done a great job of taking enough time to "just" observe them. The construction of materails does help me see and think and is motivating, but I want to make sure I'm getting better at observing.)

My work with snails is just starting to feel like "seeing with own eyes" (e.g. content of snail portrait vs. journal, Clover's race idea, shell generator idea). How do I get to this place sooner without going through the "book report" phase?
