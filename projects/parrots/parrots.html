Who are they?



Parrots are highly expressive and emotional creatures. I've develped an appreciation for them while working at an incredible parrot sanctuary, Foster Parrots, in Rhode Island.

I'm interested in how parrots react to different people and each other. Their body language is so alien to me that before really delving into why they do what they do, I've begun researching their anatomy, their vision, and their cognition and have been photographing them to get better at observing them.

Visual Study

These recordings highlight some parrot body language I find striking. I made it black-and-white because parrot colors are so beautiful that they can distract from what the parrots are actually doing.

From the few films I've seen, I'm inspired by the film maker Frederick Wiseman. His films about institutions feel quite unmediated and more like biographies than documentaries. I'd like to learn more about his films and process so that I can keep them in mind as I work to get better at showing the intelligences and emotional expressions of different animmals.
